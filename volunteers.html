The conference team

These are the people who battled bureaucracy to make the annual conference possible. In their quest to make the conference experience a memorable one, they frequently chewed each other's brains outargued with each other almost hourly; a transcript of an argument is preserved at this link.

The volunteers are listed in the Conference volunteers table. To sort the table by any column, click that column. To know more about the role of a person, click on that person's name.

In addition to the volunteers, some people contributed to this conference without being aware of doing so. Thanks are given to them in Acknowledgements.
